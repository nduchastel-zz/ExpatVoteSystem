Notes:

  • The number of 'vouchers' is the total number of person who vouched for this person;
  • To 'vouch' for someone is to say something like "yeah; this person does meet the criteria":
    1. Canadian citizen;
    2. living outside Canada; and,
    3. is 18 yrs of age or more.
  • It is possible to have 2 or more person who vouched for you already (i.e. 'Number vouchers' >= 2) and still not being certified. This occurs when there is no link to a 'master' record - see FAQ for more details
  • The 'master voter' is the main record (aka Nicolas Duchastel de Montrouge): since this is not an official web of the government of Canada, we do not have the resources to officially certify someone. We accomplish a similar mechanism by having 2 people vouch for any one voter and then, have a 'web of trust' go back to one of the Master Voters;
