Canadian Expat Voting System

The goal of this page is to 'simulate' an electronic voting system.

For now, this only focuses on Canadian citizens living outside of Canada (because, as of now, all 1.4 million of us who have lived more than 5 years outside of Canada are being denied to vote).

This is not a formal Elections Canada web site; and is in no way an official vote. It is only meant to:

  1. protest the fact that we are being denied our right to vote; and,
  2. show that this could be done (see notes below).
